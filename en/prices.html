Prices

The prices are based on your income. If you do not wish to provide a bank statement, you will be charged the fixed price of 35GBP
    Income
  • up to 800GBP (includes students) : 20GBP/session
  • up to 1200 GBP: 30GBP/ session
  • up to 1500 GBP: 40GBP/session
  • up to 2000 GBP: 50 GBP/session

Prior to the booking of the session, a deposit of 30GBP will be charged.

I request that any cancellations to our booked sessions will be made with a minimum of 48 hours' notice. Any cancellations made after this time will be charged to your deposit fee.
