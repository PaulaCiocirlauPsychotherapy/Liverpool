My name is Paula Ciocirlau

Welcome to my website. I am a licensed therapist since 2019. I am an individual member of BACP. My membership number is 0095 7245. For more information about my membership you can contact BACP or directly me.

I have completed my studies in the university of Sigmund Freud in Vienna, Austria, which is also the place where psychotherapy was born. Currently I am studying a master course in Psychotherapy and Counselling, with a Person-Centered approach, in the University of Liverpool John Moores. I can speak Romanian, English and German fluently.

I offer Systemic Counselling and Psychotherapy, as well as Person-Centered. My experience consists of working with a variety of issues. My aspiration, together with your collaboration, is to direct you into achieving your goal(s). Due to my international travels and studies, I can understand a variety of cultural perspectives. I offer a safe, non-judgemental, caring space for you to share any thoughts and feelings that you wish to remain confidential.
